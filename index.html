How accurate is AuditSmart?▾

50-60% detection rate — competitive with paid tools. We position as a pre-audit triage tool: find the obvious issues fast and affordably.

What vulnerabilities do you detect?▾

Reentrancy, integer overflow, access control, oracle manipulation, gas griefing, flash loans, selfdestruct backdoors, delegatecall exploits, signature replay, and more.

What is the deduplication engine?▾

10 agents often find the same bug. Our dedup engine merges duplicates, removes false positives, and auto-corrects severity. Clean findings, not noise.

How is this different from Slither?▾

Slither is one of our 10 agents. We layer 8 AI specialists + Gemini on top, plus deduplication, severity correction, and branded PDF reports.
